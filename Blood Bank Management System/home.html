Eligibility Criteria

Find out if you are eligible to donate blood:

  • Age: Must be at least 18 years old.
  • Weight: Minimum weight of 50 kg.
  • Health: Must be in good health and feeling well on the day of donation.
  • Medical History: No history of certain chronic illnesses or infections.
  • Transfusion: No blood transfusions in the last 12 months.
  • Medications: Certain medications may affect eligibility; consult before donating.
  • Travel: Recent travel to high-risk areas for infections may disqualify you.
  • Risk Behavior: No engaging in high-risk behaviors that could affect blood safety.
  • Pregnancy: Must not be pregnant or recently given birth (consult for specifics).
